Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about NutriSage.

How do I reset my password?

Open the NutriSage app and go to the sign-in screen. Tap “Forgot Password,” enter the email linked to your account, and follow the reset instructions we send you.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion from the app’s account settings, or email us at Support@sagegroupapps.com. We’ll confirm your request and permanently remove your account data according to our privacy practices.

How do I update the app?

Visit the App Store or Google Play on your device, search for NutriSage, and tap Update if a newer version is available. Keeping the app updated ensures you get the latest features and improvements.

How do I contact support?

Email us at Support@sagegroupapps.com. Include a short description of your issue and, if possible, your device type and app version so we can help you faster.

When will I receive a reply?

We aim to respond to all support emails within 1–2 business days. During busy periods it may take a little longer, but we’ll get back to you as soon as we can.
